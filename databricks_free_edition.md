## Sign up for Databricks Free Edition Account
1. As team leader, create a Databricks Free Edition account for team to work together.
   - Databricks Account has Workspace(s).
1. Go to https://login.databricks.com/, at the bottom, click on "Sign Up".
   - <img width="300" height="400" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7d22b8e5-6fb6-439a-89d5-263241f96b6e" />
1. Enter your email, and hit the "Continue with email" button.
   - <img width="300" height="400" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ef17e1f3-b255-47a0-b33b-05eec2d54058" />
1. Enter the six numeric code that you receive in your email.
   - <img width="400" height="180" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2ab7d1a0-55f6-458c-b0a2-5f292925173b" />
   - <img width="300" height="400" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bf10c3d0-c30d-4b4f-98e5-2ddc0696283d" />
1. Even if you have other work databricks account(s), or joined another free account(s), you can still create a personal free account by using your email.
   - <img width="280" height="450" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c69d476d-74cf-4f65-a010-39205cfbd83e" />
1. Just make sure you click on the "Get Free Edition" For personal use.
   - <img width="400" height="450" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/acb8014d-a034-4c1b-a7af-b6a43bd30a42" />
1. Enter the details
   1. Account Name, example: 99_DataHero
   1. Location, example: Canada
   2. Read the Terms of Service, click "Continue"
      - <img width="200" height="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/de481658-899a-45b7-b0fd-d43c6a267381" />
1. You are ready, if you see Databricks Free Edition Account Workspace landing page: "Welcome to Databricks".
